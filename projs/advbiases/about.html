Adventures in Cognitive Biases

This ongoing game was started by Cassandra Xia during her masters degree with the MIT Media Lab's Fluid Interfaces Group.

Warmest thanks to the friends who helped me: Dor Abrahamson, Ned Burnell, Gary Chan, Yasemin Gokce, Cory Li and Ruwen Liu, Pattie Maes, Paul Medlock-Walton, Kelly Ran, Mitch Resnick, Jacob Steinhardt, Bret Victor, Michael Wu, Victoria Xia, and Sophia Youn.

Ideas in the game were heavily influenced by two classes at MIT (9.660 by Prof. Josh Tenenbaum) and (6.437 by Prof. Lizhong Zheng) and a workshop taught by the Center for Applied Rationality.

Built with jquery/jqueryui, bootstrap, jqmath, underscorejs.
